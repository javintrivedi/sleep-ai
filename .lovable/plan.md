

# Sleep Quality Prediction Research Poster

A stunning single-screen academic poster showcasing how AI/data science analyzes biometric patterns to predict sleep quality. Designed with a subtle pastel blue color palette for a clean, professional look.

---

## Visual Design

**Color Palette**
- Primary background: Soft pastel blue (#E8F4FD)
- Accent blues: Light sky blue, muted navy for text
- Chart colors: Gradient blues with subtle coral/peach accents for contrast
- Clean white cards for data visualization sections

**Layout**
- Single-screen design (no scrolling) optimized for presentations
- Grid-based layout with clear visual hierarchy
- Elegant typography with academic styling
- Subtle shadows and rounded corners for modern feel

---

## Poster Sections

### 1. Header Banner
- Title: "Predicting Sleep Quality from Biometric Sensors"
- Subtitle: "How AI learns your body's sleep patterns before you even close your eyes"
- Clean institutional styling

### 2. Sleep Stages Chart (Area Chart)
- Shows REM, Deep, Light, and Awake phases across a night
- Stacked area visualization with pastel blue gradients
- Timeline from 10 PM to 7 AM
- Labels for each sleep stage

### 3. Heart Rate & HRV Trends (Line Chart)
- Dual-axis line chart showing heart rate and HRV patterns
- Pre-sleep → Sleep onset → Throughout night
- Annotations highlighting key prediction windows

### 4. Sleep Quality Score (Gauge/Radial Chart)
- Circular progress indicator showing predicted quality (0-100)
- Color-coded zones: Poor / Fair / Good / Excellent
- Sample prediction with confidence interval

### 5. Feature Importance (Horizontal Bar Chart)
- Ranking of biometric features by predictive power
- Includes: HRV variability, Resting HR, Body temperature, Movement, Pre-sleep HR pattern
- Percentage or coefficient values displayed

### 6. Methodology Panel
- Brief explanation of data science approach
- Key algorithms mentioned (e.g., Random Forest, Neural Networks)
- Data sources and sample size notation

---

## Technical Implementation

- Built with React and Recharts for interactive graphs
- Responsive design that fits perfectly on one screen
- Sample/demo data pre-populated for immediate presentation use
- Print-friendly styling
